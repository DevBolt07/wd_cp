Volunteer FAQs

Is there any age requirement?

Volunteers must be 16 years or older. Parental consent is needed for minors.

Is volunteering paid?

No, all volunteering is unpaid. However, you gain experience and recognition.

Can I get a certificate?

Yes! Active volunteers will receive a Certificate of Appreciation.
